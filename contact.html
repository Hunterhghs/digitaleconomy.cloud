FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope. A basic cloud migration might take 4-6 weeks, while a full blockchain protocol can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely. We have a dedicated Starter plan designed for early-stage companies. We understand startup budgets and can scale our engagement as your business grows.

What technologies do you work with?

We're cloud-agnostic (AWS, GCP, Azure), work across major blockchain networks (Ethereum, Solana, Polygon), and use modern stacks for analytics including Python, Spark, and TensorFlow.

How do you handle security?

Security is foundational to everything we build. We follow zero-trust principles, conduct regular penetration tests, and maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance. All smart contracts undergo independent audits.

Can you work with our existing team?

Yes. We frequently embed with client engineering teams. We can augment your existing capabilities, provide knowledge transfer, or work as a fully independent unit — whatever fits best.

What's included in ongoing support?

All plans include monitoring, incident response, and regular performance reviews. Growth and Enterprise plans include 24/7 support, proactive optimization, and dedicated account management.
